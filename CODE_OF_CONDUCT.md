# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. This project is an Islamic application, and we ask all participants to maintain the highest standards of respect, kindness, and professionalism.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the app's Islamic mission
- Showing empathy towards other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or content
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a mosque or Islamic gathering

## Guidelines

1. **Be Respectful** — Treat everyone with dignity and honor
2. **Be Constructive** — Offer feedback that helps improve the project
3. **Be Patient** — Not everyone has the same experience level
4. **Be Islamic** — Let the values of Islam guide your interactions
5. **Be Helpful** — Assist newcomers and answer questions kindly

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), with Islamic values at its core.
